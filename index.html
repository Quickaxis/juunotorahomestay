About the Stay

Simple. Central. Comfortable.

Junu Tora Homestay is nestled on the 3rd floor of Kadim's complex, right in front of Vishal Mega Mart at Thana Chariali, one of Dibrugarh's busiest and most convenient locations. Perfect for professionals visiting Dibrugarh for work, meetings, or university visits. Everything you need is within walking distance.
